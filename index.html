Calossa Part 1 - Yellowstone Adventures

By Leena

Chapter 1 - A mysterious force

This is a story that happened in the World War 3 which was a disastrous war happening in the 2167.

Those days near the Yellowstone lived two Chixyleen siblings named Lilasceen Chixyleen and Liandra Chixyleen. Their father went out (with a group of others) to bring peace in the world and they lived alone with their mother.

Once a message came to the Chixyleens. It read:

Tammin Chixyleen (who was their father) has got a death sentence and will be killed by tomorrow. His last wish is attached in this letter:

Goodbye... You have to find out the secret of Calossa from Yellowstone volcano and spring girls... Don't worry, You'll do very good without me

Signed by Vigind

The Chixyleens cried and cried and cried until Liandra pointed out that they should not waste time.
